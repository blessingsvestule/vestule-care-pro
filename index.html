⚕️ Medical Disclaimer

MamaShield is not a medical service. It is a personal scheduling tool to help you track your baby's Nigerian NPI immunization appointments.

MamaShield does not provide medical advice, diagnoses, or treatment recommendations. All vaccine information is sourced from Nigeria's NPI 2025–2026 schedule published by NPHCDA.

MamaShield is not affiliated with the NMA, MDCN, or NPHCDA. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical decisions. If your baby is unwell, seek immediate medical attention.

🔒 Data Privacy — Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023

What we collect: Baby's name, date of birth, sex, and optionally blood group, mother's name, and clinic name.

Where stored: Only on your device. MamaShield uses secure local device storage (IndexedDB). Your data is never transmitted to any server and survives browser cache clearing.

To erase: Use "Clear my data" inside the tracker anytime.

Data Controller: Blessing's Vestule · blessingsvestule@gmail.com

🔔 Add vaccines to Google Calendar

Tap each button to open Google Calendar pre-filled with that appointment. Then set it as an Alarm so your phone makes sound.

⚠️ Set sound alerts — very important

Android: After adding — open the event → tap "Add notification" → change "Notification" to "Alarm" → set 1 week before AND 1 day before → Save. Then go to phone Settings → Apps → Google Calendar → Notifications → set to High Importance.

iPhone: After adding — open the event → tap "Add alert" → select 1 week before AND 1 day before → Save. Go to Settings → Sounds → Calendar Alerts → select a ringtone so it rings even on silent.
