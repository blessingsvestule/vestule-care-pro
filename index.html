⚕️ Medical Disclaimer

Vestule Care is not a medical service. It is a personal scheduling tool to help you track your baby's Nigerian NPI immunization appointments.

Vestule Care does not provide medical advice, diagnoses, or treatment recommendations. All vaccine information is sourced from Nigeria's NPI 2025–2026 schedule published by NPHCDA.

Vestule Care is not affiliated with the NMA, MDCN, or NPHCDA. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical decisions. If your baby is unwell, seek immediate medical attention.

🔒 Data Privacy — Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023

What we collect: Baby's name, date of birth, sex, and optionally blood group, mother's name, and clinic name.

Where stored: Only on your device. Your data is never transmitted to any server and survives browser cache clearing.

To erase: Use "Clear my data" inside the tracker anytime.

Data Controller: Blessing's Vestule · blessingsvestule@gmail.com
